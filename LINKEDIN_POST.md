---

Excited to share my submission for the **Google Antigravity PromptWars Hackathon** — the **Election Process Educator**!

Democracy shouldn't be complicated. Yet every election cycle, millions of citizens struggle with confusing jargon, unclear timelines, and scattered information about how to vote.

So I built an **interactive AI guide** that walks you through:

- A personalized **Voter Registration Wizard** with a custom checklist
- An **Election Timeline** with key dates filtered by your location
- A **Jargon-Free Glossary** of 50+ election terms in plain English (and Spanish!)
- A **Knowledge Quiz** to test your civic readiness
- A **Polling Location Finder** with ID requirements and accessibility info
- **Social Sharing** so you can spread voter awareness

**Tech highlights:**
- Angular 19 with standalone components, zoneless change detection, and signal-based reactivity
- Node.js/Express backend with Helmet, CORS, and Joi validation
- Google OAuth, Google Analytics, and reCAPTCHA integration
- Firebase Hosting on the free Spark plan
- 100% accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA) with bilingual support (EN/ES)
- Glassmorphism UI with mesh gradients and ambient idle mode

Every citizen deserves to vote with confidence. This tool makes that possible.
